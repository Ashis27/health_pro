Avg Blood Alcohol Level Ranges

BAC : >= 0.00 & <= 0.03

Normal behavior, no impairment

BAC : > 0.03 & <= 0.06

Mild euphoria and impairment; decreased inhibitions.

BAC : > 0.06 & <= 0.10

Buzzed, euphoric, increased impairment.

BAC : > 0.10 & <= 0.20

Drunk, emotional swings, slurred speech, nausea, loss of reaction time and motor control.

BAC : > 0.20 & <= 0.30

Confused, nauseated, poor mentation, blackout.

BAC : > 0.30 & <= 0.40

Possibly unconscious, unarrousable, loss of bladder function, risk of death.

BAC : > 0.40

Unconscious, coma, impaired breathing, risk of death.
